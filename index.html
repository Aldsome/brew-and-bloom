Our story

Small batch. Big heart.

Brew & Bloom started as a weekend pop-up in Quezon City. Today, we hand-roast beans from Benguet and Sagada and partner with local pastry makers to bring you something familiar — and a little surprising — in every cup.

  • Direct-trade Filipino beans
  • Compostable cups & lids
  • Barista-led recipes, refreshed monthly
